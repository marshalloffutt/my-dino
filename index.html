Stegosaurus

The stegosaurus has always my favorite dinosaur because he had cool plates on his back for protection. In addition to these plates, he also had a barbed tail which he used to whack his opponents. It is a pretty sweet evolutionary trait. Famous stegosauruses include Godzilla. Godzilla was basically just an angry stegosaurus due to extreme radiation.
